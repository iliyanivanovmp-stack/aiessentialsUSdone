## SEO Guidelines
- Target 1,500-2,500 words per post
- Include 1 primary keyword and 3-5 secondary keywords
- Use H2 and H3 headers strategically
- Include internal links to service pages
- Add clear CTAs throughout the content
- Optimize meta descriptions (150-160 characters)

## Publishing Schedule
- Target: 2-4 posts per month
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Promote on LinkedIn within 24 hours of publishing
